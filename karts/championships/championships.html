Karting; Addictive Racing on Turbo Sliders
We are currently running two championships.
The 'Classics' championship features 12 classic tracks covering a range of styles and some very challenging circuits.
A more popular championship is the 'Sprint' series, with 14 high speed sprints to the chequered flag.




Classic Championship:
Dock Of The Bay, Rome, PC World, GTX-National, Woody Green, Test Track 020, RAC-Rally, The Suburbs, Rome City, Dagenham Dave, Simples MK3, Cross Fire, Tarmac Terry, Salt Lake City.


Sprint Championship:
In-Round, Moto-Cross, The Zone, Basildon, LongStr, GTX-Indy, Stadium, BazVegas, Brentwood, Flat, GTX-Clubman, Race Track, Woody Park, Flat 2.
